### Job Status

Jobs have many different statuses 

* Some common Job statuses include:
  * Qualifying
  * Wait to Start
  * Time
  * Dependency
  * Thresholds/Resources
  * Machine
  * Running
  * Finished OK
  * Marked Finished OK 
  * Failed
  * Marked Failed
  * On Hold
  * Cancelled
  * Skipped (or Job to be Skipped)
  * Under Review 
  * Fixed
